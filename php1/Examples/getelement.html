Smite Chest Introduction
Treasure Chests are a feature that was introduced with SMITE Version 1.0.2247, which released on August 7, 2014. They are a way to spend Gems.png Gems and try your luck to get rare or unique Cosmetic Items that can't be obtained in any other way. The treasure chest menu can be accessed from the in-game store. To open a chest, simply select the chest you want, and then click the "Unlock" button. Chests can only be opened with gems. Standard chests usually cost 400 Gems.png to open while Mystery-type chests tend to cost 200 Gems.png. Each chest has a timer which shows when it will be removed from the store. Expired chests may reappear now and then for limited periods of time. There are a few special chests exclusive to Clans that can only be unlocked with NewUI Clan Honor.png Honor. For more detailed information, please visit its main article: Clan Store
